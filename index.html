Our Package

Bali

Discover Bali, an island paradise known for its stunning beaches, lush rice terraces, and vibrant culture. Visit ancient temples like Tanah Lot and Uluwatu, experience the island's unique rituals, and indulge in world-class Balinese cuisine. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or spiritual rejuvenation, Bali offers it all.

Japan

Experience the charm of Japan, where ancient traditions meet cutting-edge modernity. From the serene temples of Kyoto to the bustling streets of Tokyo, Japan offers diverse cultural experiences. Explore Mount Fuji, the beautiful cherry blossoms in spring, and savor the delicious variety of Japanese food, including sushi, ramen, and tempura. Japan is a destination that promises both serenity and excitement.

Singapore

Visit Singapore, a vibrant city known for its modern architecture, rich culture, and fantastic food. Explore the famous Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, and indulge in street food at hawker centers.

Bandung

Explore Bandung, the charming city known for its cool climate, beautiful landscapes, and diverse shopping options. Discover the famous Tangkuban Perahu volcano, enjoy shopping at factory outlets, and savor local delicacies like batagor and siomay.

Surabaya

Experience Surabaya, Indonesia’s second-largest city, offering a mix of modernity and tradition. Visit historical landmarks such as the House of Sampoerna, explore vibrant markets, and taste local specialties like rujak cingur and lontong balap.

Osaka

Explore Osaka, Japan's vibrant city known for its modern architecture, exciting nightlife, and delicious food. Visit iconic landmarks like Osaka Castle, Universal Studios Japan, and Dotonbori, while indulging in local dishes like takoyaki, okonomiyaki, and kushikatsu.
